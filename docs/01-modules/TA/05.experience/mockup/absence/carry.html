Reset Not Applicable

This policy uses carry forward. Unused days (up to the cap) are carried to the next period rather than being reset to zero.

Days exceeding the 5-day cap will expire at year-end.

Did You Know?

This policy complies with Vietnam labor law which requires allowing unused annual leave to be carried forward for at least 3 months into the next year.
