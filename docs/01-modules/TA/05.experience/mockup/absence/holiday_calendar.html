Bridge Day Rules - Automatic gap-filling between holidays and weekends with mandatory compensation working days.

Rule 1: Weekend Extension Bridge

Auto-bridge when holiday near weekend

Condition

Holiday falls on Tuesday or Wednesday
Gap of 1-2 days to weekend

Action

Create bridge day(s) to connect
Add compensate working day (Saturday)
Example: Apr 30 (Wed) → May 1-2 (Thu-Fri)
Wed 30/4 Thu 1/5 (Bridge) Thu 1/5 + Fri 2/5 Sat-Sun OFF Sat 3/5 Work

Rule 2: Weekend Substitute

Holiday falls on weekend → substitute next working day

Condition

Holiday falls on Saturday or Sunday

Action

Create substitute day on next Monday (or Tuesday if Monday is also holiday)
Example: Tết Feb 1 (Sat)
Sat 1/2 (Tết) Mon 3/2 (Substitute)
